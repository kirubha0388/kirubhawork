About PEPS Tech

Bridging the gap between academia and industry through innovation, hands-on training, and real-world projects.

Our Story

Founded in Hosur, PEPS Tech is a leading Research & Development and Training company. We are passionate about empowering students, professionals, and organizations with the skills and experience needed to thrive in the digital era.

Our journey began with a vision to make high-quality, practical education accessible to all. Today, we offer a wide range of programs including internships, academic projects, IT training, and placement support, all designed to deliver real-world impact.

  • Live project internships for Engineering, Science, and Diploma students
  • Academic project guidance and support in latest domains
  • Corporate-style IT training in Python, Java, Embedded, IoT, Data Science, and more
  • Placement assistance, resume building, and career guidance
  • Research & Development consultancy and innovation projects

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To deliver exceptional learning experiences, foster innovation, and empower individuals to achieve their career and research goals.

Our Vision: To be a trusted partner in education and R&D, creating a community of skilled professionals and innovators who drive positive change.

"We believe in continuous learning, hands-on experience, and a commitment to excellence."
